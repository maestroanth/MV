The Multiverse says to you,



"Congratulations Sage! I don't know how you did it, but you have started making your first Universe! Now it is up to you to fine-tune it's parameters to your liking. There is no right or wrong answer since you are the God and Creator of this new Universe!"


- Directions: First Remove The Points to the Pool by deducting from your current Universe's attributes. Once they are in the point pool, you can then reassign them as you see wish. You may not modify any further after you spend the points in your point pool. However, if you want to start over and try again, just hit the "reset" button at no penalty. You may also spend some energy and take a huge hit to your chance of rolling a Faulty Universe to re-roll the random parts of this Universe's stats. You may fine-tune the attributes relative to your Sage Level; however the more you fine-tune, the higher the energy cost will be along with an increased likelihood of this Universe blowing up in your face!
