About SOMV

- Sage of the Multiverse (SOMV) © 2018 is "The Universe Trading Card App" where users take on the role as a "Sage of the Multiverse" where they can create, manage, and edit their truly own Universe cards from various websites and API's. The cards are set up with various statistics in mind as a basis for a future gameplay and game design. Once the game is built, SOMV will become "The Universe Trading Card Game" © 2018.



Only One Pre-Requisite in becoming a Sage...


The Multiverse says to you...

"There is only one requirement in order to bear the title, 'Sage of the Multiverse', and that is the ability to control one's own laws of physics."


There is no "magic" in SOMV...


"Instead of casting 'magic spells' at one another, Sages try to effactuate their own laws of physical reality on one another. Think of it this way, what if your Einstein and Schrodinger came up with a 'Theory of Everything' that explains the birth of your Universe and thus the very laws of physics in which you are enslaved to?"

The Multiverse shudders at the thought of being enslaved to such a controlling and mathematical Universe such as ours...

and then continues to speak...

"Wouldn't the next step be engineering this 'theory' to your advantage? Thus, breaking the shackles off your particular 'reality'? That's what our Sages do.....they don't duel in attempts to create merely a better world. They duel in attempts to create better realities!"


"Finally, if you are having trouble with this application, feel free to download this handy quickstart guide."


"And with that, I bid you luck, Sage of the Multiverse!"
